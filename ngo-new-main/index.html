Women Empowerment

Since our establishment in 1981, we have been at the forefront of women empowerment. Our organization is dedicated to promoting gender equality and uplifting the lives of underprivileged women.

Health & Nutrition

We have been tirelessly dedicated to the field of Health and Nutrition, championing the promotion of traditional healthcare systems with unwavering support from international NGOs.

Water and Sanitation (WATSAN)

Enhancing water hygiene in 50 villages by promoting rainwater harvesting, advocating safe drinking water, distributing Matka Filters, and conducting water quality tests. We encourage Phaydemand Sauchalaya in flood plains.

Education and Awareness

We've set up 05 N.F.E. Centres and organized 50 Adult Education centres in flood-affected areas. Our initiatives include mobile libraries, health education camps, and implementing the Right to Education Acts across 10 villages.

Child Rights

Empowering child rights, we raise awareness against child marriage and implement child protection schemes. Volunteers are educated on laws and policies, while initiatives like Childline 1098 combat child marriage, labor, abuse, and trafficking.

DRR & Climate Change

We've empowered women farmers to adapt to local climate change, fostering women leadership and resilience. Through network strengthening and Village DRR Plans, we ensure disaster resilience. We lead project teams, forge partnerships, and conduct community capacity building for disaster risk reduction in Mahishi village, including rehabilitation and flood relief efforts.

Skill Development

We provide comprehensive training to women entrepreneurs from the most disadvantaged population in Mahishi village, Saharsa district, North Bihar. Our programs cover stitching, computer literacy, composting, and business-related skills, empowering them for sustainable livelihoods and economic independence.

Sustainable Agriculture

We collaborate with local organizations to promote smart agriculture, integrating traditional knowledge (desi jugaad) with innovative approaches. Our focus is on Sri Vidhi farming techniques, enhancing agricultural productivity sustainably while respecting local wisdom and fostering innovation in farming practices.

Environment and Clean Energy

We disseminates knowledge on clean energy and distributes solar rooftop systems to promote clean and green energy among local and marginalized populations. Additionally, they take the initiative to plant trees every year, further contributing to environmental sustainability and community well-being.

Livelihood Promotion

We are fostering awareness of government schemes, mobilized youth for community support, and organized emergency flood relief in Mahishi block. Through capacity building, gender policy development, livelihood models, and goat distribution, we empower communities. Our initiatives include baseline surveys, community sensitization, Gram Sabha participation, and forming CBOs, SHGs, and Kisan Clubs.

Empowering Change, Transforming lives!

Kosi Seva Sadan is a Gandhian Organization managed by the erstwhile volunteers originated from the Jayprakash Movement during Bhoodan Andolan. Since then the erstwhile volunteer started working in Mirzapur district during Bihar Movement launched by Jayaprakash Narayan. At that time 81 Gram Sabhas( A goup of small network in villages) were formed in the Gramdani Villages of Mahishi Block. The organization follws the Gandhian Principle and facilitate the process of holistic development of society based on equity, justice and sustainability. Kosi Seva Sadan has been involved in Bharat Yatra launched by Noble Prize winner Kailash Satyarthi.
